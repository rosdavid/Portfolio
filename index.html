Hi, I'm David Ros, or simply Ros, a 23 year old Junior Front-end Developer and UX/UI Designer with an insatiable passion for technology and creativity. Since my self-taught beginnings at the age of 11 in the world of programming, I've been immersed in the exciting journey of bringing ideas to life through lines of code. My unwavering curiosity has led me to explore and learn in the realm of web development, while combining my love for music, video games and film every step of the way.

  • My Beginnings

    Since I can remember I have always been passionate about video games, so I did not hesitate to investigate in creating my own stories as soon as I got my first laptop at the age of 11 years old. It was when I discovered Unity and C#, that was my start in the world of development.

    2011 - 2016

  • Learning Web Development

    As the years went by and I did more research on the internet, I came across some videos on YouTube about web development and I became more and more interested in that world and all the possibilities it had, so I downloaded Notepad++ and started to learn the basics in HTML, CSS and later on JavaScript.

    2016 - 2018

  • My First Work

    In 2018 I started studying music production and sound engineering at UVic as it was something I loved to do, but I left it to develop the family business website about an event services company, and that's how my journey in the world of web development started. I still dedicate some of my free time to produce some music as a hobby.

    2018 - 2023

  • Where I am Now

    Now that I had advanced knowledge in web design and development, I started to make personal projects of ideas that came to my mind within the sectors I know in which I still work today, and eventually I found an opportunity to develop and design an online food and beverage store in Shopify called liquoly, where I was also in charge of creating and designing the brand image. At the same time I continue to expand my knowledge by currently learning about React and Angular.

    2023 - Now
